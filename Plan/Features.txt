Petrol Station Simulator:
	Features:
		Definitely make it Object Orientated!

		Top-Down 2D Movement: ✔
			Simple WASD movement. No Rotation.
		Customers: 
			Random AI. They should arrive in a car or walk. They need to buy something or pay for petrol.
			Pathfinding:
				The customer needs to increase their X until it reaches the destination.

		Selling Items:
			Have to stock shelves. Each Item needs to have a certain temperature.
		Buying Stock:
			Should be able to order different foods and shelves
		Editable layout:
			Can buy shelves and edit the layout of the station inside and outside. Should be able to click on objects.
